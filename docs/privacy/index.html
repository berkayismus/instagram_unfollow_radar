Privacy Policy

Instagram Unfollow Radar

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

Instagram Unfollow Radar stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • Your Instagram session data (used to make API requests on your behalf)
  • Unfollow history and statistics
  • Whitelist and keyword filter preferences
  • Your Google account name, email, and profile picture (when signed in)
  • Premium subscription status

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your Instagram password.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not store your Instagram session tokens on any server.

3. Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive your basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) via Google's OAuth API. This information is used solely to identify your account and manage your Premium subscription. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google services.

4. Payments

Payment processing is handled entirely by Paddle. We do not store your payment card details. Please review Paddle's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle payment data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth — for authentication
  • Paddle — for payment processing
  • Instagram API — to perform actions on your behalf

6. Data Retention

All locally stored data can be cleared at any time by removing the extension or resetting it from within the popup. If you delete your account from our backend, all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: support@berkayismus.dev
