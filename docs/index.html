📸 Instagram Unfollow Radar

Automatically detect and unfollow users who don't follow you back

What it does

Instagram Unfollow Radar is a Chrome extension that scans your Instagram following list and identifies accounts that do not follow you back. With one click, you can unfollow them automatically — safely and at a human-like pace.

Free & Premium

The free plan allows up to 10 unfollows per day. Premium subscribers can unfollow up to 500 accounts per day, with additional features including a watch list, CSV export, and priority support.
